ABOUT THE HUNGARIAN PARLIAMENT

The Hungarian Parliament Building is 268 metres long and 123 metres wide. With a 96 metres high dome, it's one of the two tallest buildings in Budapest. Inside the Hungarian Parliament Building there are 691 rooms, 29 staircases, and 10 courtyards. 40 million bricks were used in its construction.
